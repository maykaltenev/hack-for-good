Services

Tech Education - We introduce kids and adults to tech education using novel platforms from our partners e.g. Code it! Studio, located in Leipzig, Germany. The platforms are customized to suit the needs of different environments and the people we engage.
Training - We offer courses on how to teach coding, website building and etc for trainers in collaboration with our partners. The goal is to train facilitators to support tech education and entrepreneurship agenda of the Code for Afrika e.V.
Entrepreneurship - We organise workshops on innovative business modelling for building new and sustainable ventures. Participants are introduced to tools that support the creation of new ventures and validating existing ones.
Coaching - At Code for Afrika, provide coaching and mentoring services for startups and individuals and groups who want to create new ventures or businesses. Entrepreneurs have access to our pool of experts from Germany and Africa.
